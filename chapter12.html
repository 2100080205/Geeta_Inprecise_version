Krishna Teaching About Devotion
Krishna explains the path of pure devotion and describes the wonderful qualities of His dear devotees. He teaches about having the right attitude, being kind to everyone, and developing a loving relationship with God through devotion.

üìñ Krishna's Secret About Pure Devotion

"My dear devotees are very special to Me! They have a pure heart and always think of Me with love and kindness."

"A true devotee is kind to everyone, never gets angry, and treats all living beings with love and respect."

"When you have the right attitude and pure devotion, you become very dear to Me and I take special care of you!"

"The path of devotion is the easiest and most joyful way to reach Me. Just love Me with all your heart!"
