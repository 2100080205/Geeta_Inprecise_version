Chapter 2: The Eternal Soul 🧘

Krishna teaches Arjuna about the soul that never dies and the importance of doing our duty!

Krishna Teaching Arjuna
Krishna begins to teach Arjuna about the eternal soul, dharma, and the path of righteous action. He explains that the soul is immortal and that we must do our duty without attachment to results.

📖 Krishna's Wisdom

"The soul is eternal and never dies. Just like we change clothes, the soul changes bodies."

"A wise person is not happy or sad about things that come and go. They stay calm and peaceful."

"Do your work with love, but don't worry about getting rewards. Just do your best!"

"When your mind is steady like a lamp in a windless place, you will find true peace."

📊 Main Things to Learn from This Chapter

🧘G: GURU (Verses 1-10)
Krishna becomes Arjuna's teacher and begins to guide him with wisdom and compassion.
👤 I: IDENTITY (Verses 11-30)
Krishna teaches about the eternal soul - who we really are beyond our physical body.
⚖️ T: TWO DHARMAS (Verses 31-53)
Krishna explains Svadharma (our personal duty) and Nityadharma (universal principles).
🕉️ A: ATMARAMA (Verses 54-72)
The qualities of a person who has found inner peace and joy in the soul.

🌟 What We Learn

👤 Our soul is eternal and never dies
⚖️ We should always do our duty with love
🧘 Stay calm and peaceful in all situations
🎯 Work without attachment to results
💎 True wisdom comes from understanding the soul

Verse 2.20

"The soul is never born and never dies. It has always existed and will always exist."

Verse 2.22

वासांसि जीर्णानि यथा विहाय नवानि गृह्णाति नरोऽपराणि

"Just as we put on new clothes and throw away old ones, the soul takes new bodies and leaves old ones."

Verse 2.47

कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन

"You have the right to do your work, but never to the fruits of your actions."

Verse 2.70
