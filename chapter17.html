Chapter 17: FAITH FLOWS THREE WAYS 🌈

Shraddhā-Traya-Vibhāga-Yoga – The Division of Faith!

Krishna Teaching About The Division of Faith
Krishna explains how faith, food, sacrifice, and charity can be sattvic (pure), rajasic (passionate), or tamasic (ignorant) – and how our faith shapes our future!

📖 Krishna's Secret About Field and Knower

"Your body is like a field where things grow, but you - the soul - are the farmer who knows about the field!"

"True knowledge means understanding the difference between your body (which changes) and your soul (which never changes)."

"A wise person sees that the same soul lives in all bodies - in you, your friends, animals, and plants!"

"When you understand this secret, you become free from all worries and find true happiness and peace!"

🌾 Main Things to Learn from This Chapter

🅕 F : FAITH
Krishna explains that every person has faith, but it is of three kinds: Sattvic – pure, peaceful, and directed toward truth and God. Rajasic – passionate and for personal gain or show. Tamasic – ignorant, harmful, and superstitious.
🅐 A : AUSTERITIES
💡 Sattvic austerity is done with faith, not for praise. 🔥 Rajasic is done to show off. 😣 Tamasic is painful and harmful, often without proper knowledge.
🅒 C : CHARITY
💎 Sattvic – Given at the right time, place, and to the right person, without expecting anything. 💰 Rajasic – Given with expectations or pride. 🙈 Tamasic – Given without respect, at the wrong time, or to unworthy causes. 📖 Krishna teaches that pure charity uplifts both the giver and the receiver.
🌟 E: Enjoyer
Krishna reveals the sacred syllables that make actions pure: 🔔 OM – chanted to start any spiritual act. 🙏 TAT – done as a selfless offering to the Supreme. 💫 SAT – truthfulness, eternal goodness.

🌟 What We Learn

🌾 Your faith defines your path
👁️ Eat sattvic food to nourish body and mind
🧠 Charity and rituals should be pure and selfless
🤝 Remember Om Tat Sat while doing good deeds
🌟 Whatever you do, do it with true intention and faith

Verse 17.3

सत्त्वानुरूपा सर्वस्य श्रद्धा भवति भारत। श्रद्धामयोऽयं पुरुषो यो यच्छ्रद्धः स एव सः॥

"O Arjuna, the faith of every person is shaped by their nature. A person is what their faith is."

🍎

Verse 17.7

आहारस्त्वपि सर्वस्य त्रिविधो भवति प्रियः। यज्ञस्तपस्तथा दानं तेषां भेदमिमं शृणु॥

"Even food is dear to each person according to their nature. So are sacrifices, austerities, and charity – all are of three types."

💎

Verse 17.20

दातव्यमिति यद्दानं दीयतेऽनुपकारिणे। देशे काले च पात्रे च तद्दानं सात्त्विकं स्मृतम्॥

"Charity given at the proper time, place, and to the worthy without expecting anything – that is sattvic charity."

🔔

Verse 17.23

ॐ तत्सदिति निर्देशो ब्रह्मणस्त्रिविधः स्मृतः। ब्राह्मणास्तेन वेदाश्च यज्ञाश्च विहिताः पुरा॥

"Om Tat Sat – these three words are names of the Supreme, and by them, the ancient sages performed yajnas and studied the Vedas."
