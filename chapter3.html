Chapter 3: The Path of Action üå≥

Krishna teaches Arjuna about doing work without attachment and the importance of selfless action!

Krishna Teaching About Action
Krishna explains to Arjuna that everyone must act, but we should work without being attached to the results. Like a tree that gives fruits freely, we should do our duties with love and without expecting rewards.

üìñ Krishna's Wisdom on Action

"Everyone must do some work. Even I, who need nothing, continue to work for the good of all!"

"Do your work like offering flowers to God - with love, but don't worry about getting anything back."

"A person who works without selfish desires becomes free and happy, like a bird flying in the sky."

"When you help others and do good work, you become closer to God and find true joy."
