Krishna Teaching About Divine Presence
Krishna reveals how He is present everywhere - in every sound we hear, in every person we meet, and in everything we see. He teaches about accepting or rejecting God, and the rare devotion that makes us truly special.

üìñ Krishna's Teaching About Divine Presence

"Listen carefully! You can hear Me in every sound - in birds singing, in water flowing, and in your friends laughing."

"I am everywhere around you - in the sun that warms you, in the flowers that smell sweet, and in every loving heart."

"Some people accept Me with love, while others reject Me. But I love everyone equally, like a mother loves all her children."

"True devotion is very rare and special. When you love Me with all your heart, you become the most precious person to Me!"
